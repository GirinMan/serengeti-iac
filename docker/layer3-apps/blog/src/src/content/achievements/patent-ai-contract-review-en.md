## Patent Scope

This patent covers the end-to-end pipeline for AI-powered contract review:
- **Contract Analysis**: Clause-level extraction, party identification (97.5% accuracy), and automatic contract type classification
- **Summarization**: Structured contract summary generation with key term identification
- **Checklist Answering**: Custom playbook-based AI review — clause-level risk assessment with Green/Yellow/Red risk ratings
- **Model Training**: Legal domain-specialized LLM fine-tuning and evaluation framework

## Production Deployment

The technology described in this patent has been running in production for 2.5 years as the allibee contract review AI product, supporting PoC deployments across 4 enterprise clients, 38 contract types, and achieving a 91–93% fix ratio for suggested revisions.
