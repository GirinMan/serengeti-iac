## Presentation Content

LangCon is the leading developer conference for the Korean LLM/NLP community. Presented under the name `Seongjin Lee (BHSN)`, at BHSN, a legal AI startup (product: allibee):
- **Long-context tuning**: Open model fine-tuning strategies for processing legal contracts of 54K–80K tokens
- **Document summarization & RAG**: Legal document summarization and multi-turn RAG chat pipeline design
- **LoRAX-based serving**: Production architecture for efficiently serving task-specific LoRA adapters via multi-adapter operations
- **Serving efficiency strategies**: Production operational know-how including prefix caching, structured output, and GPU memory optimization
